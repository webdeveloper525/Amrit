The Story of My Story

When I was in 2nd grade, I would love to write stories. But these stories were extremely short. They were not written very well, and they would be extremely boring to read. I took a break from writing stories. Then, I started to play a lot of Minecraft with my friends. My best friend would always do some roleplays with me. Then, in one of the roleplays, he made up a character named Lord Evilious. But in my mind, I really didn't like this name, but I liked a name called the Evil Lord. Just by making up the character, I thought about the fact that I could make a whole plot, with the Evil Lord being the antagonist. But I didn't want this book to be a mini book, like the ones that I created in second grade. I wanted this to be a book that had many themes to it. I wanted to make an incredible book. But this idea didn't last for long. Soon, I forgot most of this idea, until one day.

I went to India with my cousin last year. One day, I asked her what she was doing. She said she was writing a story. I read the story. It had many descriptive details, leading me to love this book. This immediately inspired me to write my own book. I took the idea that I had a long time ago, and I made it into a story.

By next year, I had finished the first book, and I was working on a rough draft for the second book. But I had started to become bored working on it, so I stopped working on it. But when Quarantine happened, I decided to work on the first book again. I decided that it needed a lot of revamping. 3 months later, I finished it! It came out much better than I thought it would. I am currently working on finishing my second book. Creating my book is one of my greatest accomplishments.
